Alan Turing

Early life: Born in London, educated at Cambridge and Princeton; showed early brilliance in mathematics and logic.

Major contributions

  • Formal model of computation (Turing machine)
  • Foundational paper on computable numbers & the halting problem
  • Codebreaking work at Bletchley Park (WWII)
  • Proposed an operational test for machine intelligence (Turing Test)

Impact on modern AI

  • Inspired research on limits of computation and intelligence
  • Influenced early symbolic AI and later ML philosophy
  • Framed key questions about learning, reasoning, and language
